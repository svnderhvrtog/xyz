How to start?
Place your bet in the left panel, you can choose between 1CR, 2CR or 5CR. Press the 'confirm'-button. You can now start the game Blackjack.

Scoring
If you beat the dealer, you win double your bet. If you lose, you forfeit your entire bet. If your hand ties with the dealer's, the round ends in a push, and your bet is returned.

How Blackjack works
Blackjack is a card game where the objective is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. You start with two cards and can choose to draw additional cards or stick with their current hand.

The aim is to outscore the dealer without going over 21. If your hand exceeds 21, you "bust" and automatically lose the round.
